Summary: Latitude vs. X

This project compared latitude with several variables such as temperature, humidity, wind speed and cloudiness. These weather factirs provide us insight that could support a variety of research. From the visualizations, we have found that the only only weather factor that showed a strong correlation in the data was City Latitude vs. Max Temperature. This study in particular lead to an observation that as latitude increased, temperature decreased.
